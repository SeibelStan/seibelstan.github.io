How much

It depends on how much time can be given to the project, accordingly, the volume of the functional, the detailization of the study, involved specialists. Can be used as the first solutions, in-depth study of the subject area or unique algorithms development.

Many projects are not a product, but a process. In this case, the quality of the team determines the success of a business on a par with the owner.

There are tasks that a high-class specialist can do quickly; the average specialist will take much longer; the weaker can not. The high rate of specialists means that the project will be executed quickly and efficiently, without a significant increase of cost.

Usually around a few hundred dollars per team member, and full project is done by forces of 3-4 people.

  • Senior developer, 20+$/hour - can determine what to do, lead a project, has good skilled in algorithms and technology stack, rich experience in solving problems of different subject areas.
  • Middle, 15$/h - well performs the tasks on the scale of the project, can make a study of the subject area.
  • Junior, $10/h - can do small corrections, that does not require knowledge of the subject area and the niceties of the technology.

The irony is that all these classes can be one person in different conditions.
